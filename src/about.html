ABOUT US

Cartoon Hangover is a Frederator online animation channel. Cartoon Hangover launched in 2012 as a part of the YouTube Original Channel Initiative, and now also has a channel on the streaming platform VRV.

Frederator produced two animated series exclusively for the channel: Bravest Warriors, created by Pendleton Ward and Bee and PuppyCat, created by Natasha Allegri. Previously, they produced SuperFuckers, created by James Kochalka. Frederator also produced two cartoon shorts incubators for Cartoon Hangover: Too Cool! Cartoons and GO! Cartoons.

Developing original content and high quality animation, Cartoon Hangover champions independent artists.

Cartoon Hangover is now available on VRV and YouTube.
